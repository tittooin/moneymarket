Networks and variants

RuPay, Visa and Mastercard variants differ; some cards include Priority Pass or DragonPass for international entries.

FAQs

How many entries do I get?

Entries are often capped per quarter; add-on cards may have separate limits.

What should I carry?

Keep your card, boarding pass and ID; confirm terminal lounges that accept your network.
